2. What Credit Card Stacker Does

Credit Card Stacker is an educational credit card planning tool. It helps users manually organize credit card information and understand general credit concepts.

CalculatorsUtilization, paydown targets, card-by-card balances, and reporting estimates based on user-entered data.
RemindersStatement date, due date, and weekly check-in reminders shown inside the app.
EducationLessons, glossary terms, quizzes, and beginner explanations about credit cards.
Card categoriesGeneral categories like secured, starter, cashback, and travel cards. These are not approval decisions.

The app relies on information entered by the user. If user-entered information is incomplete, outdated, or wrong, calculations and suggestions may also be inaccurate.

You should make your own financial decisions and consult qualified professionals when needed. You are responsible for reviewing card terms, fees, APR, lender requirements, and legal disclosures before applying for any credit product.

4. User Responsibility

You agree that you are responsible for:

  • Entering accurate card balances, limits, due dates, and statement dates.
  • Verifying payment due dates directly with your card issuer.
  • Making payments on time through your actual bank or card issuer.
  • Reading all card terms before applying.
  • Understanding that app reminders may not always be delivered or seen.
  • Not using the app as the only source for important financial decisions.

Credit Card Stacker cannot make payments for you, cannot guarantee payment processing, and cannot confirm that a card issuer reported a balance to any credit bureau.
