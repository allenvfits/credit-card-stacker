Utilization Help

Utilization is your balance divided by your credit limit. It is one of the main things this app helps users understand.

Example: $250 balance ÷ $500 limit = 50% utilization.
A common beginner target is under 30%.
A stricter rebuilding target is under 10%.
The app shows how much to pay to reach your selected target.
Lower utilization may help your profile look cleaner, but this app does not guarantee any specific credit score increase.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions users may ask.

Does the app pull my real credit score?

No, not in this MVP. The app uses a manually selected estimated score range. Real credit score access would require a proper credit data provider, consent flow, and stronger legal/security setup.

Does this app guarantee approval?

No. Credit Card Stacker never guarantees approval. Lenders make their own decisions based on full applications and credit profiles.

Is this credit repair?

No. The app is an educational credit card planning tool. It does not dispute credit reports, remove negative items, or promise score changes.

Why do statement dates matter?

The balance around the statement closing date may be what reports. Paying before the statement closes may help a lower balance show on the statement.

Where is my data saved?

In the demo version, data is saved in your browser using localStorage. It can be exported or deleted from Settings.

Does Premium charge real money?

No, not in this HTML demo. Premium is simulated in your browser. Real billing requires Stripe, Apple, Google, or another payment provider.

Troubleshooting

Fix common issues while testing in VS Code or Live Server.

⚠️
My dashboard is empty

Add cards in onboarding or dashboard. You can also click Load Sample Cards on Dashboard, Reminders, or Reports.

🧹
I want to reset the app

Open Settings, go to Data Management, and click Delete All App Data. This clears the local demo data.

🖥️
Links do not open correctly

Make sure every HTML file is in the same folder and the filenames match exactly, like dashboard.html and card-finder.html.

🔐
Sign-in is not real yet

auth.html uses a demo local session. Connect Supabase Auth later for real accounts and login security.

Premium Help

Premium is currently a demo feature saved in the browser.

Open premium.html to view the pricing page.
Click Start Premium to open the demo checkout popup.
Click Simulate Upgrade to turn on demo premium.
Open settings.html to turn demo premium on or off anytime.
For real subscriptions, create checkout sessions on the server using Stripe or use App Store subscriptions. Never put secret keys in HTML.
