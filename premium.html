FAQ

Subscription questions.

Does Premium guarantee my credit score will go up?

No. Premium gives planning tools, reminders, and educational guidance. It does not guarantee score increases, approval, or lender decisions.

Does this page charge real money right now?

No. This is a front-end demo. The checkout button only simulates premium status in your browser. Connect it to Stripe Checkout later.

How would Stripe connect later?

The Premium button should call a server-side endpoint or Supabase Edge Function that creates a Stripe Checkout Session. After payment, your Stripe webhook updates the user profile to premium.

Can users cancel?

Yes. Once real billing is connected, add a billing portal link so users can cancel or manage subscriptions clearly.

Legal-friendly billing copy: Premium is an educational planning tool. It is not a credit repair service, lender, bank, credit bureau, or financial advisor. Premium does not guarantee credit approval, specific score changes, or better credit terms. If real card links or affiliate offers are added later, disclose compensation clearly near each offer.
